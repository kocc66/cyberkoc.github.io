Tufan Berber

Author & Student
Born in February 2008 in Istanbul as the first child of a family with two children. He completed his primary education in Ümraniye. He is currently studying at Dr. Vasıf Topçu Science High School in Şile, Istanbul. In May 2024, he stepped into authorship with his book titled "Sultan II. Yasin Emir Koç

Student & Programmer
Yasin Emir Koç was born in Istanbul and is currently continuing his education at Dr. Vasıf Topçu Science High School. He attended Deneyap Türkiye, receiving training in technology and engineering, but did not complete the program. During his middle school years, he developed two different projects within the scope of TEKNOFEST, focusing on domestic violence against women and energy production. Interested in innovative ideas and socially beneficial work, Yasin Emir aims to create impactful projects in the fields of technology and innovation in the future.

About Us

1D1P Collective is a groundbreaking publishing initiative that brings together writers from around the world to create a unique anthology of human experiences. Our mission is to preserve diverse voices and stories in a timeless collection that will be cherished by future generations.

Founded in 2025, our project has already collected over 0 unique stories from 0 countries. We believe everyone has a story worth telling, and our platform makes it possible for anyone to contribute to this global literary tapestry.

Each page in our collection represents a $1 contribution, making this project accessible to everyone while ensuring the highest quality publication. Our team of editors and designers work tirelessly to curate and present these stories in beautiful limited-edition volumes.
